### Q: "How do you typically collaborate with AEs? How do you keep sales and technical aligned?"

### Q: "An AE brought you into an unqualified deal — how did you handle it?"
**Principle: qualify hard, protect everyone's time, stay a good partner.**

If two of those are missing, I'll say so directly and suggest we either reframe the scope or focus the energy on a more qualified opportunity. Walking away from a bad POC early protects the AE's quarter and our credibility with the buyer. My kill criteria are: no clear technical win, no access to a real environment, no engaged champion, or a hard requirement the product genuinely can't meet."
