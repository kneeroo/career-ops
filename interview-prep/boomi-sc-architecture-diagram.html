The line that lands it: "One platform handles real-time order flow, scheduled batch, human approvals, and a resilient queue for the flaky warehouse — decomposed into independent processes that share common connections, profiles and maps. You're not buying four tools, you're buying one control point for how your business data moves."

Why processes, not one monolith

Each flow has its own trigger and lifecycle, so it's its own process — independently deployable, testable, monitorable, with isolated blast radius.
